What is a threefold Farmer?

ThreeFold Farmers are individuals and organizations that help to build more future-proof and sustainable internet infrastructure by connecting 3Nodes(servers) to the ThreeFold Grid – the world’s largest peer-to-peer network of Internet capacity (compute-, network- & storage capacity).  

Why you should consider becoming a farmer

You become a contributor in a movement towards a more sustainable and neutral Internet grid

You can bring Internet capacity to places where it is currently not available yet

You help protect new and future users of the Internet on multiple levels

You become part of the ThreeFold Token’s circular economy simply by running a 3Node (server) from your home or office
